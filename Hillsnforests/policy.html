Resort Policies

Stay informed, travel stress‑free. Know our policies before your stay.

Cancellation & Amendment Policy

  • No charges if informed 7 days prior to check-in.
  • 100% charges apply if informed less than 7 days.
  • Between Dec 24 – Jan 1, a 15-day policy applies.
  • Refunds take 10 working days with 5% gateway charges.

Resort Stay Policies

  • Check-in: 1:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in or late check-out = Full-day charges
  • Suites are double occupancy only.
  • Triple occupancy: Extra charges apply if guest is over 7 yrs old.
  • No extra bed for guests below 7; bedding on diwan provided.
  • Swimming pool: Complimentary. Bring swimsuit, cream, lotion.
  • No doctor on call. Nearest hospital: 4km.

Dining Policy

  • Meals included: Breakfast & Dinner at La Providence
  • No redemption through in-room dining.
  • Meal Timings:
    • Breakfast: 7:30 AM – 10:30 AM
    • Lunch: 12:30 PM – 3:30 PM
    • Dinner: 7:30 PM – 10:30 PM
  • 24/7 room service available for à la carte orders.

Guest Guidelines

  • No non-occupants allowed inside suites.
  • Reusable flip-flops are for suite-use only. Available for purchase at The Verandah.
  • Do not remove reusable suite items.
  • Damage to property will be chargeable.

Important Notes

  • Please read resort policies before arrival.
  • Violations may result in being asked to vacate.
  • Pre-book trek, yoga, or activities in advance.
  • Suites are for 2 adults (1 king bed + 1 couch).
  • No outside food or liquor allowed.
  • No pets allowed.
  • Images on site are for marketing only.
  • This is a Heritage Resort Project approved by Govt. of India.
